This paper was written for a major assignment in English 201. The objective was to research a topic and present it in an informative way for a general-education audience. I chose to explain how modern artificial intelligence systems work at a conceptual level.

  • Google Docs Link: The Intelligence Behind Artificial Intelligence: How Neural Networks make Intelligent Decisions

Prompt

Write a conceptually accurate paper explaining how artificial intelligence works for an audience with no background in computer science or mathematics.

Major Things I Had to Learn Going Into the Project

  • How Neural Networks function
  • The Backpropagation Algorithm
  • Gradient Descent and the Role of Learning Rate in Parameter Updates
  • How Transformers Work and Why They Are Preferred
  • What Numerical Stability Is

Technical Takeaways

  • Neural networks can be understood as layered generalizations of classical regression models, where weights and biases are learned through optimization
  • Transformers incorporate positional information and rely on self-attention instead of recurrence, which allows for parallelized training
  • Although transformer architectures can be slower on single-core systems, their design enables efficient scaling on modern multi-core and distributed hardware

Reflections

  • There is an inherent tradeoff between technical precision and accessibility when communicating complex topics to a general audience
  • Effective explanations require selectively omitting or simplifying details without distorting the core concepts
